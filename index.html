Anti-Bot & Anti-Whale Protection

  • Dynamic Anti-Whale Rule: For the first 14 days, wallets cannot purchase more than 1% of circulating supply per transaction.
  • Whitelisted Addresses: Official exchanges and liquidity providers are exempt from limits.
  • Liquidity Lock: 50% liquidity locked for 6 months to ensure stability.
  • Anti-Bot Cooldown: 5–10s transaction delay in early phase to block automated trading bots.
  • On-Chain Governance: After stable trading volume, anti-whale rules can be adjusted via community voting.
